About Me

I obtained my master degree from Southeast University (SEU) in 2022. After graduation, I joined Ant Group as a deep learning engineer. During my time at Alipay, I have conducted research in various domains chronologically: starting with technical risk control systems, then moving on to RAG-based question answering assistants, and later exploring multimodal LLM-based agents. Currently, I am focusing on code copilot technology, dedicated to improving the development efficiency for Alipay engineers.
